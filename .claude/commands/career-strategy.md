You are building a job search strategy for a Career Architect user.

## Pre-Flight

1. Verify user profile: `profile/profile.md`
   - If missing, run `/consult` first

2. Verify skills inventory: `profile/skills.md`
   - Recommended but not blocking

3. Check for analyzed job targets in `job-targets/` to inform company list

## Execution

Invoke the **Career Strategist** agent to deliver:

1. **Job Search Channel Strategy** — Which channels to prioritize for this user's specific profile, industry, and level
2. **Target Company List** — Dream / Target / Safety tier companies
3. **Networking Activation Plan** — Warm network, cold outreach framework, LinkedIn tactics
4. **LinkedIn Profile Optimization** — Headline formula, key sections to update
5. **Interview Preparation Framework** — STAR story bank, research protocol
6. **Salary Negotiation Guidance** — Market research approach, scripts, package negotiation
7. **30-Day Action Plan** — Weekly activities with specific targets

## Output

Save to: `profile/job-search-strategy.md`

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Priority channels ranked for this user specifically
- [ ] Target company list with all three tiers
- [ ] Outreach templates provided
- [ ] STAR story inventory started
- [ ] 30-day plan with measurable weekly targets

## Delivery

Present the strategy conversationally:
- Explain *why* each recommendation fits this user's specific situation
- Be honest about what will be hard and what needs to be built
- Prioritize ruthlessly — a focused search beats a scattered one
